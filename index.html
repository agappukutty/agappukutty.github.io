About Us

We offer live, virtual 1:1 career advisory sessions to anyone interested in getting a head start in searching for their next journey in life.

  • All 1:1 sessions are 30 minutes can be used however you prefer
  • Sessions are free of charge; upon completing your session, you will be asked to make a donation
  • All donations (minus costs) are used to fund our current campaigns; min. recommended donation is $10 USD per session

At Career Ark, we want to help you help us. By offering free 1:1 career advisory sessions, we hope our experiences and insights can help prepare you for success, however you define that. Our time is just as valuable as yours, so all we ask in return is for monetary donations. Though the minimum recommended donation is $10 USD, we understand everyone's situation may be different. We trust you will do the right thing. If you are interested in scheduling a session with one of our specialists, click below!

Schedule A Session

Our 1:1 sessions are led by you, giving you the freedom to use the time however you like

These sessions can be used for any purpose. Want live resume feedback? We can provide it. Need interview prep? We can help. Sessions are designed and led by you to maximize the use of our time together. Learn below how the process works.

  • 01 Choose your desired focus (e.g., business, medical, engineering)

    We offer career advisory services in three focus areas: business, medical, and engineering. Choose the focus you wish to seek help for.

  • 02 Select your specialist and schedule time

    We have a breadth of specialists with diverse experiences eager to help you! Choose the one that fits your needs best and schedule time via calendly. While scheduling, you can enter information to help your specialist understand more about what you are hoping to gain.

  • 03 Have the 30-minute 1:1 session

    Use the session however you like! The time can be used for almost anything. Common uses include resume reviews, interview preparation, application advice, and more. Though these are common uses, we can be used for almost anything!

Frequently Asked Questions

See below for common questions prospective users have. If you have other questins, feel free to reach out to us at admin@careerark.com.

  • Who can sign up?

    The simple answer is anyone! But to make the best use of our time together, we typically work with college-aged students or individuals older to provide meaningful and relevant professional and educational advice.

  • What can I use the time for?

    You can use the time however you like. Upon scheduling time, you can share a few thoughts to let your expert know how they can serve you best. During the session, you lead the time and your expert will help you live!

  • How are sessions conducted?

    For now, our virtual sessions are conducted through Google Meet, but we are working to expand this into other video conferencing platforms.

  • When are sessions conducted?

    Our sessions are conducted throughout the week at any times based on expert availability. In the scheduling process, you will be directed to your expert's calendly, where you can find time to schedule a 30-minute session.

  • Can I sign up for another session?

    We love hearing positive feedback and always welcome this! As we value time, our only ask is that after each session, you make a donation to one of our current campaigns, so we can continue making a lasting impact.

  • How do I make a donation?

    Upon completing your session, you will recieve an email to make a donation to the cause of your choosing. You can always donate on our "campaigns" tab at any time. We accept donations in the form of Venmo or Paypal. We appreciate all your support!
