About Us

We offer live, virtual 1:1 career advisory sessions to anyone interested in getting a head start in searching for their next journey in life.

  • All 1:1 sessions are 30 minutes can be used however you prefer
  • Sessions are free of charge; upon completing your session, you will be asked to make a donation
  • All donations (minus costs) are used to fund our current campaigns; min. recommended donation is $10 USD per session

At Career Ark, we want to help you help us. By offering free 1:1 career advisory sessions, we hope our experiences and insights can help prepare you for success, however you define that. Our time is just as valuable as yours, so all we ask in return is for monetary donations. We recommend you make a minimum donation of $10 USD though we understand everyone's situation may be different. If you do, the entire donation minus costs of running this page will be sent to another charitable organization of your choosing. If you are interested in scheduling a session with one of our specialists, click below!

Schedule A Session

Our 1:1 sessions are led by you, giving you the freedom to use the time however you like

These sessions can be used for any purpose. Want live resume feedback? We can provide it. Need interview prep? We can help. Sessions are designed and led by you to maximize the use of our time together. Learn below how the process works.

  • 01 You choose your focus area (e.g., business, medical, engineering)

    We offer career advisory services in three focus areas: business, medical, and engineering. Choose the focus you wish to seek help for and share some details about how you would like to use the time.

  • 02 We will match you based on your prefences

    We have a breadth of specialists with diverse experiences eager to help you! We work internally to match you to the one that fits your needs best. From there, we leave it to the two of you to get started.

  • 03 Schedule and have a session

    Our specialists who we personally matched to fit your profile will reach out within 24-48 hours with a quick intro and calendly for you to select the time of your choice. From there, the two of you will conduct the session!

Frequently Asked Questions

See below for common questions prospective users have. If you have other questins, feel free to reach out to us at admin@careerark.com.

  • Who can sign up?

    The simple answer is anyone! But to make the best use of our time together, we typically work with college-aged students or individuals older to provide meaningful and relevant professional and educational advice.

  • What can I use the time for?

    You can use the time however you like. Upon filling out our form, you can share a few thoughts to let your expert know how they can serve you best. During the session, you lead the time and your expert will help you live!

  • How are sessions conducted?

    For now, our virtual sessions are conducted through Google Meet, but we are working to expand this into other video conferencing platforms.

  • When are sessions conducted?

    Our sessions are conducted throughout the week at any times based on expert availability. In the scheduling process, your matched specialist will send you a calendly link, where you can select a time for a 30-minute session.

  • Can I sign up for another session?

    We love hearing positive feedback and always welcome this! As we value time, our only ask is that after each session, you make a donation to one of our current campaigns, so we can continue making a lasting impact.

  • How do I make a donation?

    Upon completing your session, you will recieve an email to make a donation to the cause of your choosing. Of course, you can always donate on our at any time! We accept donations in the form of Paypal. We appreciate all your support!
